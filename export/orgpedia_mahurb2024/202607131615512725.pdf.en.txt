The government's decision:
A very serious accident occurred in the Pimpri Chinchwad Municipal Corporation's Waste-to-Energy Project area in Moshi on 08/07/2026 date when a garbage dump on SLF (Sanitary Land Field) collapsed and fell on the administration building due to heavy rains. A large number of people were injured in the accident.
A high-level vigilance committee is being constituted as follows to conduct an objective, impartial, in-depth and technical investigation of the accident, to investigate the causes of the accident, fix responsibility and suggest measures necessary to prevent such incidents in the future.
a) The Vigilance Committee: -

(a) The powers of the Committee: -
It shall have the power to call for and examine necessary records, files, contracts, administrative and technical clearances, maps, drawings, budgets, inspection reports, environmental clearances, CCTV footage, drone surveys, photographs, video footage, e-mails, correspondence, WhatsApp messages, digital records and other necessary documents from any department, office, project, contractor, project management consultant (PMC), consultancy body or other relevant agencies of the Municipal Corporation.
The Committee shall have the power to conduct a site inspection of the accident site as well as the area concerned, recommend necessary measurements, photography, video filming, collection of samples and conduct separate technical inspections as required.
The Committee shall have the power to call upon the concerned officers, staff, contractors, Project Management Consultants (PMCs), advisory bodies, experts or other concerned persons to attend the inspection, record their statements and call for necessary clarifications and documents.
4. The Committee shall be empowered to receive technical guidance, views or reports from other expert bodies as may be required.
The Committee shall have the power to make or recommend, as may be necessary, any Geotechnical, Structural, Environmental, Survey, Laboratory Tests or other technical examination of the site concerned.
6. The Committee shall have the power to demand any additional information, if deemed necessary in connection with the inquiry, to call the persons concerned again for a hearing and to take such other action as may be necessary for the effective completion of the inquiry.
All the heads of departments, officers, staff of the Municipal Corporation, concerned contractors, Project Management Cells (PMCs), Advisory Bodies and other concerned agencies are obliged to cooperate fully with the Committee.
