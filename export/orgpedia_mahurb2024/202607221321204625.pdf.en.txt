8.1 Types of asset monetization:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| Type                                                   | Explanation                                                                                                                                | Mode of operation                                                                                                |
| The lease-based model                                  | Monetization of urban local body property by leasing it out to a private partner over a long period of time.                               | Lease - Develop - Operate (LDO) Lease - Renovate - Operate (LRO)                                                 |
| Concession-based model.                                | Transfer of powers to operate and maintain the properties of an urban local body for a fixed period of time.                               | Operate-Maintain- Transfer (OMT) Augment-Operate- Transfer (AOT) Design-Build-Finance- Operate- Transfer (DBFOT) |
| Revenue sharing model.                                 | Urban local bodies to receive a certain share of the revenue generated from the use of the property.                                       | Management Contract Revenue Sharing Concession                                                                   |
| Joint Partnership-based Development Monetization (JDI) | Joint development of assets on a dividend basis by investing as a common partner between public / private partners and urban local bodies. | Joint Venture (JV) Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)                                                                 |
| Service-linked model.                                  | Monetization of property in respect of services provided to citizens by an urban local body.                                               | Performance-Based Contracts Service Concession Agreements                                                        |
| Structured-finance based model.                        | Monetization that securitizes on the basis of cash flow from the asset.                                                                    | Securitisation SPVs, Credit-Enhanced Bonds Infrastructure Investment Trusts (InvITs)                             |
