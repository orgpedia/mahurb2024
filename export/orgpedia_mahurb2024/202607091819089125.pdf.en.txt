The Proposal -
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 has been implemented throughout the country with effect from 17th April, 2017 and the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 1995 has been repealed. Under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016, 21 categories of persons with disabilities have been included.
Section 34 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 prescribes four per cent reservation for persons with disabilities in direct service and promotion. The reservation is aimed at empowering the differently-abled.
In order to achieve this objective, posts in the government, semi-government, local self-government institutions, autonomous bodies, corporations, etc. need to be reviewed and demarcated under Section 33.
Action must be taken according to the principle that there will be no injustice to any disabled person while performing this procedure, and the efficiency of the department will not be adversely affected. For this purpose, the Department of Disability Welfare has issued Reference No. Uniform operating procedures (SOPs) have been prescribed under Section 6.
